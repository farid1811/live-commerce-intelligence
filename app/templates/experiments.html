Scientific Evaluation & Validation Metrics
R-Squared (R²): Explains the proportion of variance in the dependent variable (sales) predicted by the features. Values closer to 1.0 indicate superior explanatory power.
Mean Absolute Error (MAE): Measures average absolute prediction error in product counts. Smaller values represent higher precision.
Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE): Penalizes larger prediction errors disproportionately, acting as a critical metric for evaluating high-variance sales sessions.
Mean Absolute Percentage Error (MAPE): Expresses absolute error as a percentage, reflecting performance across streams of differing sizes.
